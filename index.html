Formspree Setup Guide

Formspree

Step 1: Get Your Formspree Endpoint

1. Log in to your Formspree account

2. Create a new form or find your existing form

3. Copy your form endpoint URL

// Your form endpoint will look like this:
https://formspree.io/f/xldnolzz

Step 2: Configure Your HTML Form

Update your form action attribute with your Formspree endpoint:

<form action="https://formspree.io/f/xldnolzz" method="POST">
  <input type="text" name="name" placeholder="Your Name" required>
  <input type="email" name="email" placeholder="Your Email" required>
  <textarea name="message" placeholder="Your Message"></textarea>
  <button type="submit">Apply Now</button>
</form>

Step 3: Verify Your Email

1. After creating your form, Formspree will send a verification email

2. Click the verification link in that email

3. This ensures Formspree can deliver emails to your inbox

Unverified emails cannot receive submissions!

Troubleshooting Guide

Common Formspree Issues and Solutions

1

Emails not received

Possible solutions:

  • Check spam/junk folder
  • Verify your email in Formspree
  • Ensure your form action URL is correct
  • Check Formspree dashboard for submissions
2

Form submissions not showing in dashboard

Possible solutions:

  • Check browser console for JavaScript errors
  • Verify form action URL is correct
  • Test with a simple form (no JavaScript)
  • Check network tab for failed POST requests
3

Emails going to spam

Improve deliverability:

  • Authenticate your domain with Formspree
  • Add SPF and DKIM records
  • Use a professional email address
  • Whitelist noreply@formspree.io
4

Form submission errors

Debugging steps:

  • Check browser console for errors
  • Test with Formspree's example form
  • Verify required fields are included
  • Disable form validation temporarily
5
